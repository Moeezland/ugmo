About UGMO

The United Global Micronations Organization (UGMO) is an international alliance of recognized micronations. It serves as a platform for political cooperation, collective security, and structured inter-micronational relations.

Charter & Governance

Democratic Voting

Most decisions are determined by majority vote, requiring over 50% approval.

Membership Rules

Nations involved in active wars or extreme negative relations are not eligible for membership.

Leadership

A new Head of UGMO is elected every six months by member states.

Member States

UGMO consists of independent micronations from across the world. A full, updated list of member states will be published here.

Contact

For membership inquiries or official communication, please contact UGMO through its official Discord server or designated representatives.
